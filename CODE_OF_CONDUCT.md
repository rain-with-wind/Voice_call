# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative space.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful in language and tone
- assume good intent while giving clear feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior rather than people
- help make the project accessible to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- insulting or demeaning comments
- discriminatory language or behavior
- deliberate intimidation
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers through the repository issue tracker or the contact method documented by the maintainer.

All good-faith reports should be reviewed respectfully and handled with care.
